About us

Our Aim

At What To Cook?, we believe that everyone has potential to be a chef. Whether it's at a restaurant or at home, every kitchen holds endless possibilities, especially when it comes to leftovers. Founded by a passionate group of foodies, our website is dedicated to helping new chefs transform ordinary leftovers into meals that delight.

What we offer

Through our collaborative community and inventive cooking tips, we empower you to be a confident and sustainable chef capable of whipping up a good meal with anything. Join us to explore how you can elevate your cooking and use leftover ingredients in delicious and unexpected ways. We can't wait to help you start your chef journey!
